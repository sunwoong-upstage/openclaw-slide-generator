# Research Prompt Templates

Short query templates for `web_fetch` to gather credible, recent information for slide content.

## General Research

- "Latest trends in {topic} 2025 2026 overview"
- "{topic} key statistics data"
- "{topic} best practices guide"
- "{topic} beginner introduction"
- "{topic} expert deep dive analysis"

## Audience-Specific

### Executive / C-Suite
- "{topic} business impact ROI case study"
- "{topic} industry report McKinsey Deloitte"
- "{topic} market size growth forecast"

### Technical / Engineers
- "{topic} architecture design patterns"
- "{topic} performance benchmarks comparison"
- "{topic} implementation tutorial example"

### Students / Learners
- "{topic} explained simply"
- "{topic} analogies examples"
- "{topic} common misconceptions"

### Researchers / Academics
- "{topic} recent papers arxiv"
- "{topic} literature review survey"
- "{topic} methodology framework"

## Fact-Checking

- "{claim} fact check"
- "{claim} criticism counter arguments"
- "{statistic} source verification"

## Credible Domains

Prefer sources from: .edu, .gov, arxiv.org, nature.com,ieee.org, acm.org, mckinsey.com, deloitte.com, gartner.com, stackoverflow.blog, github.blog, official documentation sites.

Avoid: content farms, unverified blogs, outdated (>5 years unless historical context) sources.
